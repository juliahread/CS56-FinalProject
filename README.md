## Description and Controls
### Backstory
It's the year 2200, and the Claremont Colleges have successfully migrated to space... But over-enrollment in CS 5 at HMC has caused a singularity to form, and the Claremont Colleges have been destroyed! You must reach Campus Safety in order to survive. Good luck!
### Controls
+ Left Click - shoot the grappling hook
+ Right Click - detach the grappling hook
+ WASD - control the jetpack
+ ![alttext](https://github.com/juliahread/CS56-FinalProject/blob/master/images/apple.png "Fuel") - additional fuel
